Our Services

Buyer Home Inspection

This is the home inspection that everyone needs to get when they are buying a home. It includes a point by point examination of the home particularly focusing on Roofing, Exterior, Structure, Insulation, Ventilation, Heating, Cooling, Electrical, Plumbing and Interior.

Seller Home Inspection

Having a pre-home assessment can be a major advantage if you are planning for major/small repairs. You can do the repairs on your terms—not the buyers. For example, if your home inspector finds a leak under the sink, you can settle it. Doing numerous repairs, yourself can spare you a great deal of cash.

Mold Inspection

Mold is just one of the many issues that affect the air we breathe. We do thorough investigation of the structure to determine its current condition relative to the possibility of fungal contamination. Consequently, it's best to look for the inspection through our services mold inspection/testing and swab testing.

Radon Inspection

The colorless, odorless and radioactive gas that seeps up from the earth and is the 2nd leading cause of lung cancer in Canada. Protect yourself now by testing your home, reducing your exposure and protecting your loves ones. Be sure to check your home this winter for Radon.

Asbestos Testing

Asbestos is a naturally occurring mineral composed of thin, tightly packed fibers. Due to its strength, asbestos used to be commonly used in manufacturing of insulation, fireproofing, and other construction materials. The environmental protection agency has began regulating materials containing asbestos. Asbestos has been found to pose serious health risk.

Air Quality Test

Most of us now spend majority of our time indoors, sleeping, eating, working and socializing. Recent research tells us that we now spend up to 90% of our time indoors. Indoor pollution sources that release gases or particulates into the air are the primary cause of indoor air quality problems in homes and that can be inspected by our air quality.
